Founder's Message

Wings of Youth, Power of Purpose

"True progress is measured not by the height we reach ourselves, but by the number of people we help lift along the way. NayePankh is a collective force giving hope to the neglected."

NayePankh Foundation started as a humble group of youth determined to alleviate misery. Based out of Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, the foundation has expanded its network to include over thousands of youth volunteers across multiple cities in North India.

Registered with the government and verified under sections 12A & 80G, we ensure that every single donation is directly optimized to aid children's education and support sanitary pads manufacturing and distribution.

Prashant Shukla
Founder & President, NayePankh Foundation
